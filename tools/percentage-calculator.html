Percentage Formulas
Basic Percentage Formulas:
  • X% of Y: (X ÷ 100) × Y
  • X is what % of Y: (X ÷ Y) × 100
  • X is Y% of what number: X ÷ (Y ÷ 100)
Percentage Change Formulas:
  • % Increase/Decrease: ((New Value - Original Value) ÷ Original Value) × 100
  • Increase by X%: Original × (1 + (X ÷ 100))
  • Decrease by X%: Original × (1 - (X ÷ 100))
Percentage Difference:
  • % Difference: (|Value1 - Value2| ÷ ((Value1 + Value2) ÷ 2)) × 100
